Pack Light We're Catching A Flight!

When traveling trvalers usually over-pack! Lets stop that! A few ways we can keep ourselves from over packing, is to be sure we are bringing shoes, and accessories that can be worn multiple times. Additionally we can plan to wear only two colors for the full trip, and then all of our shoes, and accessories will match!

Look Before You Book!

Be sure to look at all fine print details when booking a flight or hotel. Make sure you, and your family are covered with travel insurance, always expect the unexpected! Make sure you are a fair distance away from the airline and your accommodation. Double check all you times to save you a dime on any missed flights or over booked hotels!
