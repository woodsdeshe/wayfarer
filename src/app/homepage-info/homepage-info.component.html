Pack Light On a

Flight!

When traveling travelers usually over-pack! Lets stop that! A few ways we can keep ourselves from over packing, is to be sure we are bringing shoes, and accessories that can be worn multiple times. Additionally we can plan to wear only two colors for the full trip, and then all of our shoes, and accessories will match! Pack all essential clothing in in your carry on incase anything happens to your checked bags.

Look Before You

Book!

Be sure to look at all fine print when booking a flight or hotel. Make sure you, and your family are covered with travel insurance, always expect the unexpected! Make sure you are a fair distance away from the airline and your accommodation. Double check all your times to save you a dime on any missed flights or over booked hotels!

Chill or Feel The

Thrill!

Always organize your trip plans before the trip so you can chill. Make sure you book all of your excursions before hand. Be sure you have sorted out your transportation needs as well. Booking excursions early can also help you navigate what you need to pack. Any popular restaurants you have on your list make sure you have booked them in advanced so that you are sure you will have a seat at the table.
